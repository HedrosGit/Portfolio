My Little Planet

Working as a token specialist on the design of a game from scratch was one of the most challenging yet rewarding experiences of my career. I was part of a team composed mostly of Russian and Ukrainian developers, which made communication a significant obstacle. However, our shared passion for game development and the determination to create something unique helped us overcome these barriers and collaborate effectively.

As a token specialist, I was responsible for designing the in-game economy, developing tokenomics, and creating a rewarding system that incentivized players to stay engaged. This involved a deep understanding of blockchain technology, smart contracts, and the specific mechanics of the game. The complexity of this task was compounded by the fact that the game was being built from scratch, which meant that we had to create everything from the ground up.

Communicating my ideas to the team was not always easy, but we quickly found ways to work around the language barriers. We relied on visual aids, such as diagrams and sketches, and often used Google Translate to facilitate communication. Despite the challenges, the team was incredibly supportive and open to new ideas, which made the collaboration process smooth and productive.

One of the biggest rewards of our hard work was the successful acquisition of investments for the company. Our innovative token design and unique in-game economy caught the attention of several investors, and we were able to secure funding that allowed us to continue our development work. This was a testament to the hard work and dedication of the team, and it was a proud moment for all of us.

Overall, working as a token specialist on a game design project was an exhilarating and challenging experience. It allowed me to push my boundaries and develop new skills while collaborating with a talented and dedicated team. The final result, a successful token design and a game that players loved, was the ultimate reward for all of our hard work.
