Working as a game designer for Dorfer on Sorting Beads: Stencil Fill was an incredible experience. This hyper-casual game challenged me to create an engaging and satisfying puzzle experience for players who enjoy color-matching games.

Sorting Beads: Stencil Fill is a fun and relaxing puzzle game where players must match and sort colored balls to fill in stencils. As a game designer on this project, I was responsible for designing the gameplay mechanics, creating the level progression, and ensuring player engagement throughout the game.

One of the most rewarding aspects of working on this project was seeing how players responded positively to the gameplay mechanics and level progression. The game's simplicity and ease of use drew players in, and the satisfying feeling of filling in the stencils kept them engaged and coming back for more.

Working with the team at Dorfer, I gained valuable experience in game design, user engagement, and project management. The collaboration with my colleagues and the opportunity to work directly with clients was a highlight of my experience with Dorfer.

Overall, working on Sorting Beads: Stencil Fill was an incredibly positive experience that allowed me to develop key skills in game design and project management. I am grateful for the opportunity to have worked on such a fun and engaging game, and I am confident that the skills I developed will serve me well in my future work in the game development industry.
